Our Unbelievable History!!

Once upon a time, not very long ago, in a place nobody really cared about, some people decided, "Hey, let’s open a resturent!" and just did it. Nobody knew why, but here we are, still open and serving food that might or might not be edible.

We started with like, 2 spoons, 3 plates, and a fridge that barely works. Our first customer ordered 5 items but only got 2 because someone forgot to cook the rest. But we persevered (somewhat) and now have a grand total of 7 regulars who come by for free snacks!

Our secret recipes are so secret even we don't remember what they are. Sometimes we guess, sometimes we experiment, sometimes we just hope for the best. But hey, it’s all about experience, right?

Through many… interesting years, we've managed to not shut down (yet!). We proudly serve food that surprises, delights, and occasionally confuses. Join us if you dare, or just scroll past this page and pretend we never existed!
